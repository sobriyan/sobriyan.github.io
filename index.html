Resources

I Miss My Cafe

I Miss My Cafe is a website that provides cafe ambiance for you to listen to as background noise while you read. There are different toggles and options to choose from ranging from machinery noise to rainy day ambiance. Moreover, the website is equipped with a Spotify playlist, to-do list toggle, and even a timer. Click the hyperlink above to go to I Miss My Cafe!

Wikiversity Annotation Guide

If you prefer to keep your thoughts ordered while you read, you might want to try annotations. This is a method of note-taking and thought organizing used by various readers to connect ideas, keep track of key events, or analyze text. Wikiveristy has an excellent baseline for annotations, which can be accessed by clicking the link above!
